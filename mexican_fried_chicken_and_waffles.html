MEXICAN FRIED CHICKEN AND WAFFLES

Sweet and savoury is an irresistible combination. Our take on Fried Chicken and Waffles will have coming back for seconds and thirds.

PREP TIME:

25 Minutes

TOTAL TIME:

55 Minutes

COOK TIME:

30 Minutes

SERVERS:

4 people

INGREDIENTS

For Chicken:
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 pound of chicken tenders
  • 1/4 a cup of HERDEZ® Avocado Hot Sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 bag of HERDEZ® White Corn Tortilla Chips
For Waffles:
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/3 a cup of HERDEZ® Avocado Hot Sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 cup of beer (optional)
  • 1/4 a cup of butter: melted
  • 1/2 a cup of chives
  • 1 cup of cheddar cheese: shredded
For Toppings:
  • 2 tablespoons of butter: softened
  • 1/2 a cup of syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of chives: chopped
  • 1/2 a cup of HERDEZ® Avocado Hot Sauce

DIRECTIONS

For Fried Chicken:
  1. Smash the HERDEZ® Tortilla Chips in a large zip lock bag and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken tenders with chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk the 2 large eggs, and HERDEZ® Avocado Hot Sauce together.
  4. Drench the chicken tenders in the wet mixture, then coat them in the smashed HERDEZ® Tortilla Chips.
  5. Air fry 4 chicken tenders at a time, at 390 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes, flipping them halfway.
For Waffles:
  1. In a large bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt.
  2. Then create a well in the middle of the dry ingredients, pour in the 2 large eggs and whisk them in.
  3. Whisk in the beer (optional), HERDEZ® Avocado Hot Sauce and melted butter.
  4. Stir in the cheese and chives into the mixture.
  5. Pour some of the mixture into a waffle iron on the medium heat setting for about 7 to 10 minutes to cook.
For Plating:
  1. Butter the waffles and place 2 chicken tenders on top of the waffles.
  2. Drizzle HERDEZ® Avocado Hot Sauce and syrup on top.
  3. Garnish with chives and now it's ready for serving.

(NOTE: This recipe servers 4, with one serving being a large waffle with 2 chicken tenders)
