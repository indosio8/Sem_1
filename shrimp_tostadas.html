SHRIMP TOSTADAS

It is loaded with black beans, lettuce, avocaado and, shrimp.

PREP TIME:

20 Minutes

TOTAL TIME:

50 Minutes

COOK TIME:

30 Minutes

SERVERS:

4 people

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 tostadas.
  • 2 tablespoons of HERDEZ速 Chipotle Cremosa Salsa
  • 1 cup of canned fat free refried beans: warmed
  • 1 cup of romaine lettuce: shredded
  • 2 tablespoons of HERDEZ速 Traditional Guacamole Spicy
  • 1 cup of corn kernels: roasted
  • 16 pieces of jumbo shrimp: shelled, deveined, and grilled
  • 2 tablespoons of queso: crumbled

DIRECTIONS

  1. Spread the tostadas with beans.
  2. Then layer each tostadas with lettuce, corn, shrimp, and the HERDEZ速 Traditional Guacamole Spicy.
  3. Top it off with the HERDEZ速 Chipotle Cremosa Salsa, and crumbled queso.
  4. Now it's ready to be served.
