MEXICAN EGGS BENEDICT

These common brunch staple gets a Mexican makeover with this fun recipe. HERDEZ® kicks up the heat with chorizo and a detectable ancho chili hollandaise sauce.

PREP TIME:

20 Minutes

TOTAL TIME:

45 Minutes

COOK TIME:

25 Minutes

SERVERS:

4 people

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 ounces of Mexican chorizo: cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 a cup of HERDEZ® Guacamole Salsa
  • 2 English muffins: split and toasted
  • 4 eggs: poached
  • 2 tablespoons of cilantro leaves: chopped
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 of a teaspon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • 1/4 a cup of butter: cubed
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1/8 of a teaspoon of ancho chili powder
  • 1 avocado: sliced

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a small saucepan over low heat, whisk together 2 large egg yolks, water, and lemon juice.
  2. Continuosly stir the mixture as it cooks for 1 to 2 minutes or until the mixture begins to bubble.
  3. Then gradually stir in butter cubes until the butter is incorporated and the sauce has thicked.
  4. Remove the sauce from the heat, then stir in salt and ancho chili powder forming the Ancho Hollandaise sauce.
  5. Divide the chorizo evenly over each English muffin and place a desired number of avocado slices then a poached egg on top of each.
  6. Spoon a desired amount of the Ancho Hollandaise sauce over each poached egg.
  7. Lastly, top with the HERDEZ® Guacomle Salsa, and garnish with cilantro.
  8. Now it's ready to be served.
