CHIPOTLE CARNE ASADA QUESADILLAS

The authentic flavours of HERDEZ TAQUERIA STREET SAUCE® Chipotle Medium shine in this tasty quesadilla recipe. These recipe make 6 large quesadillas the can serve 6 or be cut into triangles and served as appetizers.

PREP TIME:

15 Minutes

TOTAL TIME:

50 Minutes

COOK TIME:

35 Minutes

SERVERS:

6 people

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil: separated
  • 1 1/2 pounds of of carne asada
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 red bell pepper: diced
  • 1 white onion: diced
  • 3 cups of cheddar cheese: shredded
  • 6 burrito-style flour tortillas
  • 3 cups of mozzarella cheese: shredded
  • 1/2 a cup of HERDEZ TAQUERIA STREET SAUCE® Chipotle Medium
GARNISH
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Mexican crema
  • HERDEZ TAQUERIA STREET SAUCE® Chipotle Medium

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a large skillet, over medium-high heat, cook the carne asada in 1 tablespoon of olive oil for 8-10 minutes, flipping it halfway or until the internal temperature is 145 degrees.
  2. Then remove the carne asada from the heat and add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and cook the red bell pepper and white onion for 5 minutes.
  3. While the red bell pepper and white onion are cooking, slice the carne asada into bite size pieces.
  4. Use the butter to grease the griddle and one side of each tortilla.
  5. Then add equal parts of cheese, carne asada, bell peppers and onions onto to the unbuttered side of each tortilla.
  6. Drizzle 2 tablespoons of HERDEZ TAQUERIA STREET SAUCE® Chipotle Medium on each tortilla.
  7. Then fold each tortilla in half and cook them in batches in the griddle over medium heat for about 5 minutes, flipping the halfway.
  8. Cut the quesadillas into triangles and drizzle additional HERDEZ TAQUERIA STREET SAUCE® Chipotle Medium, and Mexican crema over the top then sprinkle with cilantro.
  9. Now it's ready to be served.
